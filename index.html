At FreeKapani, we believe in the power of giving back. That’s why we offer premium drinking water – absolutely free – transforming each bottle into an opportunity for brands to engage, inspire, and connect with people in a meaningful way. Our unique model merges philanthropy and sustainability, creating a dynamic new marketing channel where every sip supports a purpose.

Our Vision

We're reimagining advertising for a new era. Just as Google and Instagram deliver impactful connections online, FreeKapani bridges the physical and digital worlds, turning our eco-friendly water packaging into valuable advertising space. Every bottle is a canvas for brands to reach their audiences – whether it’s through interactive QR codes that open augmented reality experiences, shopping opportunities, food orders, exclusive coupons, prizes, event tickets, videos, music, app downloads, and more.

Commitment

At FreeKapani, we go beyond water distribution. We are committed to social initiatives that make a real difference: Water Tanks for Communities: We provide clean water access to those in need, helping communities stay healthy and thrive. Education & Empowerment: Through our partnership with Musubi-te Foundation, we support projects in rural education and women’s empowerment, investing directly in the brighter future of underserved areas.

Engagement & Innovation

We inform, inspire and explain our client’s visions for the future through their innovation & engagement projects: delivering solutions that are exciting and actionable, signalling their leadership position to customers and enhancing partnerships.
